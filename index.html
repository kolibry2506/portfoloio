Hang is there, it will get easier in a bit

Hello! My name is Lena. Made in Ukraine, I now live in Italy. I am a person born in the 1980s and a graduate of KhAI (I'm not sure if that means anything to you 😅)

I work in the back office of a commercial department and I absolutely love my colleagues - girls, you are the best!

On weekends, when I'm not feeling lazy and the weather is nice (and I'm not a very lazy person, plus Italy gets around 300 sunny days a year), I go hiking in the mountains. Most of the time, I'm with my friends, sometimes with my happiness (hello, my love!), and sometimes alone.

On this site, you'll find descriptions of some of my hikes - I hope they give you a few ideas on how to make your legs hurt for a week while enjoying breathtaking views as a bonus!
